Our Story

Founded in 2020, Jaya's Organic started as a small family business in Chennai with a simple mission: to preserve and share authentic Tamil recipes using only the finest organic ingredients. What began as homemade pickles for family and friends has grown into a beloved brand serving customers across Chennai.

Every product is made with love in our kitchen using traditional methods passed down through generations. We source our ingredients directly from local organic farms, ensuring freshness and quality in every bite.

Our journey began when our founder, Jaya, discovered the healing power of traditional Tamil herbs while caring for her family. What started as simple home remedies evolved into a passion for creating authentic, preservative-free food products that not only taste incredible but also support health and wellness.

Today, we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots. From our signature Pirandai Thokku to our unique herbal podis, each product tells a story of tradition, quality, and the love we put into every batch.

Our Story

From Jaya's Kitchen to Your Table

Founded in 2018, Jaya's Organic started as a small family business in Chennai with a simple mission: to preserve and share authentic Tamil recipes using only the finest organic ingredients. What began as homemade pickles for family and friends has grown into a beloved brand serving customers across Chennai.

Every product is made with love in our kitchen using traditional methods passed down through generations. We source ingredients directly from local organic farms in Tamil Nadu, ensuring freshness and quality in every bite.

From our signature Pirandai Thokku — which has been in our family for over three generations — to our unique herbal podis, each product tells a story of tradition, quality, and care.
